Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take? ▾
Lagos and Abuja: 1–2 business days. Other states: 3–5 business days. Express delivery options available at checkout.
What is your return policy? ▾
We offer a 7-day hassle-free return on all products. Items must be unopened and in original packaging. Contact us to initiate a return.
Are all products authentic? ▾
Absolutely. We source directly from authorised distributors and brand partners. Every product carries a genuine seal and batch number.
How do I track my order? ▾
Once your order is shipped, you'll receive an SMS and email with a tracking link. You can also contact us on WhatsApp with your order reference number.
Do you deliver nationwide? ▾
Yes! We deliver to all 36 states and the FCT. Delivery times and fees vary by location — you'll see exact costs at checkout.

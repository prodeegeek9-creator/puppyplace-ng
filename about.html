Our Story

Started With One Dog and a Dream

PuppyPlace.ng was founded in 2021 in Lagos by a team of pet lovers who struggled to find quality, affordable pet products in Nigeria. Importing from abroad was expensive, local shops were scarce, and delivery was unreliable. We knew there had to be a better way.

So we built it. Starting with dog food and a handful of suppliers, we grew into Nigeria's most comprehensive online pet store — carrying everything from premium kibble and vet-grade supplements to luxury beds and GPS trackers. Today, we ship to all 36 states and the FCT.

But we're more than just a store. We connect pet owners with verified breeders, trusted vets, and a growing community of animal lovers across the country. Because every pet deserves the best — regardless of where their owner lives.

Our Mission

Making Pet Care Better for Nigeria

Our mission is simple: ensure that every pet in Nigeria is healthy, happy, and well-cared for. We do this by partnering with trusted global brands, supporting local breeders and vets, and keeping our prices fair — because we believe quality pet care shouldn't be a luxury.
